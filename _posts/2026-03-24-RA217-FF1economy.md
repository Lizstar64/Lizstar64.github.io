So, I've beaten FF1 a few times now. Already mastered it, even. So what's this, exactly? Well, this is a romhack of FF1 where it's... FF1 again, but there's no stores.

That's it.

So basically, it's FF1. But okay, so the way it works is this: the game is unbelievably rough in the first 2/5ths or so, until you get enough items from dungeons that the last 3/5ths are just Final Fantasy 1. But UNTIL then, no pure potions, no potions in general, if you're inflicted with Stone you should just reload frankly.

![Game](https://imgur.com/o9R547A.png){: .center-block :}

Is this worth playing over FF1? No, obviously not. Is it even worth playing if you just wanna replay FF1? Uh, well... if you really like FF1? Which I do. I just kinda wanted an NES RPG to grind out while watching Youtube videos. Which this is even more that than FF1. So, hell yea. Got what I wanted.
