WHAT FUCKING TONE ARE YOU TRYING TO REACH?

Check the DNA on his neck. Check memory engrams. Fucking say "Hey computer, who was the last person to enter sick bay". YOU SHOULD BE MORE WORRIED ABOUT THIS.

Anyways Burnham gets orders to blow up a resergant camp. She claims she'll go down and take them out by herself for the glory. She then surrenders to them IMMEDIATELY and begs them to let her save them, which they obviously are uninterested in hearing, but after Mirror Sarek comes out and mind melds with her, they immediately change their plans. The leader of this group is Voq, remember him? The pale Klingon? He's speaking in Klingon and it's freaking Tyler out.

Oh also there's lots of stuff about Burnham asking Voq why he's teamed up with all these other peoples, so she can figure out how to get the Klingons to the peace table back home. Uh... cause otherwise it's death?? Burnham confronts Tyler about this, and he FINALLY awakens, and it turns out... he's Voq! Who was turned into a Human through a violent ritual, in order to infiltrate the ship. He DOES have the "real" Tyler's memories and thoughts but he's also Voq. 2.5/10

<img src="https://imgur.com/v7uv9Wo.png" alt="trek">

Meanwhile Burnham takes Lorca to the Emperor, who is VERY happy Burnham is okay, calling her daughter. A bit after caressing her in a very Toxic Mommy Empress kinda way. Damn, killed that one before I could enjoy it.

Anyways turns out Mirror Burnham betrayed the empress so she sentences her to death too. Whoops! But she convinces the Empress not to kill her by covincing her she's from the Prime Universe. The Empress is like "Huh, how quaint, parallel universes?" and then kills all of her primary guard except for one with the fucking thing that blue guy from Guardians of the Galaxy uses. Thank you, my empress, how... kind.

<img src="https://imgur.com/icQPoRC.png" alt="trek">

Stamets finds out that Culber is dead, because apparently a ghost image of him was dragged into the Fungus Network Forest. Okay. His fucking boyfriend became a force ghost leading him back to his body. Also apparently Mirror Stamets did evil shit to the mycellial network and corrupted it. This killed all the fungus on the Discovery, so they're VERY stuck now.

The Empress talks to Burnham about what Mirror Lorca was apparently like... He was her father figure, but groomed her. They literally use that word, groomed. It was... gross. All the things she explains HOW he groomed her, we get twists from past scenes showing he's STILL doing it. This is how Burnham realizes the Twist.

LORCA IS FROM THE MIRROR UNIVERSE.

I guessed this a long-ass time ago btw. And immediately after she realizes this, Lorca puts on the Villain Hat VERY easily, getting out of the excrutiator, and killing people. Better late than never I guess.

Lorca being evil is a twist that like... makes you recontextualize this series a little bit. Cause like, he was ALWAYS bad and evil all along, so all the evil shit he's doing is justified, right? No. Obviously not. He's been in the prime universe for a long time and they just Let Him Do That. It was never treated as bad. That's the problem.
