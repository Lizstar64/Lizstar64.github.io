**Perpetual Infinity** - DIS
2/10

<img src="https://imgur.com/NvOrICJ.png" alt="trek">

We get a flashback showing Michael's childhood, and the death of her parents. But obviously not. She wakes up, having been fully resucitated. She's told her mother is unconscious on the planet, but alive.

<img src="https://imgur.com/qRtRoNs.png" alt="trek">

The section 31 guy I could never remember the name of is captured by the evil AI and injected with some kind of things, with a very graphic and violent scream. It's gross. RIP that guy I guess. His corpse is taken over and controlled by the AI. Why does Section 31 have borg-esque nanotech??? It's actually BETTER, cause no one realizes it, it's subterfuge.

Michael watches the mission logs from her mother's suit. She sees that she tries to get back over and over but fails. And also she's in a post apocalypse. Eventually she switches to trying to fix this problem instead. Michael is kinda pissy she's focusing on trying to fix this issue and not see her. Come on, Michael. Dr. Burnham is like "Just delete the sphere archive, that'll solve ALL of this" "No, we're protecting it!" "Then we're done here." She's become a distant Time Protector type character.

They actually DO try to delete it, but the data itself refuses to be deleted. 

The section 31 guy's corpse is a lot more malevolent than usual. But everyone's just ignoring it. If anything I'd say this is extremely accurate to how people in power misuse that power and everyone who has the ability to stop them just doesn't. He teams up with Georgiou and asks her to help kill Dr. Burnham AND steal the sphere data. Please don't fall for this shit, Georgiou.

<img src="https://imgur.com/0Fux9RW.png" alt="trek">

They come up with a plan, though. They're gonna upload the sphere data into the suit, and send it into the future so it survives, AND is away from the evil AI. They'll also use some dark matter shit to keep her here.

Georgiou goes up to Dr. Burnham and all the guards just let her, and as she's clearly menacing, they just stand there and watch. Why are they even here? After realizing that hey, maybe what she's doing is OBVIOUSLY dumb, she stops the transfer and sends Ash over to stop Leland. Ash is stabbed. Whoops. Maybe you should have DESTROYED the thing downloading the data??? 

<img src="https://imgur.com/TiQp6ZR.png" alt="trek">

Leland goes down guns blazing to get the data, deactivating the suit. They release the containment field keeping Dr. Burnham there, and she returns to the future, without the suit this time, stuck in the future. But hey, the AI doesn't have all the data I guess.

Things are distraught, but for some reason this clears the air between Michael and Spock so they're on speaking terms again?? Also Ash escaped in an escape pod.
