Another day, another tiny homebrew that's basically just a demo. This time it's Niñoid, by Three Twins Games. It's a metroidvania I decided to play while still kind of in a Metroid mood. I played it right after Junkoid, which I didn't master, but might talk about someday. 

![Game](https://imgur.com/4snaUpm.png){: .center-block :}

Anyways, Niñoid wears its inspirations on its sleeve, as you can see from this screenshot. You play as a kid chasing after his cat. You go into some ancient cat temple and get cat themed upgrades to proceed. There are only like ten rooms. Like I said, it's more of a demo than anything else. You can beat the game in under 2 minutes. But for whats here, it's cool? I wish the devs would do more with this engine!
